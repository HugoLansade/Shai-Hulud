Introduction

You embrace now the almighty Shai Hulud. The biggest sandworm of Arrakis. As a protector of your land, you will have to destroy Harkonnens, Sardaukars and their vehicules. You will win point of life by doing so. Nevertheless, be carefull of bombs that are drop on or under the sand.

How to play ?

You can use arrows keyboard to slide under the sand.

Chapter 1
